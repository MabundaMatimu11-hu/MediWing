Our Drone

Flytrex

This drone is designed to deliver medicine quickly over short distances. It takes off vertically, flies directly to the patient's location, and stores limited medication. It is programmed with GPS to follow a safe flight path to its destination.

VTOL Drone

This is a VTOL (Vertical Take-Off and Landing) drone. It is fast, stable, and capable of traveling long distances more efficiently than regular quadcopters. It has a large storage that can carry up to 150–250 kg of medical supplies.

DroneUp

This drone is designed to deliver medicine quickly over short distances. It takes off vertically, flies directly to the patient's location, and stores limited medication. It is programmed with GPS to follow a safe flight path to its destination.

SkyRelief

This drone flies through the air and releases the medication using a small parachute, which gently drops the package to the ground. It can travel up to 80 km one way and works in various weather conditions, including light rain and wind.

SkyPharm

This drone transport essential medication efficiently and safely over long distances. it can travel up to 25–40 kilometers on a single charge, depending on weather conditions and terrain. It can hold and deliver up to 2–5 kilograms of medication.
